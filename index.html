BERKELEY BUILDS
ABOUT STUDENTS SPONSORS

Berkeley Builds is a social innovation designathon on UC Berkeley's campus that invites students from all academic backgrounds to implement sustainable solutions to social issues both domestically and internationally. Through collaboration with a diverse leadership of student-run organizations focused on a range of topics from social impact to technology to consulting, we encourage students to design solutions to address real problems that non-profit organizations face.

We aim to maintain a mutually beneficial relationship with the non-profit organizations we collaborate with. The students gain hands-on experience and have the opportunity to produce significant impact while networking with potential future employers. At the same time, non-profit organizations receive free tailored solutions and innovative ideas as well as the opportunity to market their organization, advocate for their cause, and meet potential recruits.
